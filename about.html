About us

We uphold the highest standards of quality in our products,ensuring durability and precision in every machine and die we produce.

HTMT PVT LTD is a venture started in the year 1995 as a Sole Proprietary firm with its business roots established at Hyderabad, Telangana (India). Our company is indulged in the manufacture and export of an extensive range of high-quality Machining Tools and equipment. Our product entity comprises Thread Rolling Machine, SS Thread Rolling Dies, Roll Turning Lathe Machine, MS Spline Rolling Machine, MS Bar Threading Machine,. Also, we provide Thread Rolling Job Work, Thread Rolling Machine Maintenance Service,.These products are manufactured employing latest technically arbitrated machines installed in our premises for better precision and accurate results. Our product line meets all major quality parameters of the industry and immensely satisfies our esteemed customers. We are also catering to the international market with our strong distribution network. We also impart maintenance services for our product line.
